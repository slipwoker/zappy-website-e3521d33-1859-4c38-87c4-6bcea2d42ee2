FAQs

Quick answers for quick time.

Is this made for short breaks?

Yes—layouts prioritize fast scanning and minimal friction. Every game is one click away, with no complex menus or registration walls. Perfect for squeezing in a quick session between classes.

Can we suggest new games?

Yes—send titles and the reason students ask for them. We're always looking to expand our collection with games that students actually want to play. Your feedback shapes what gets added next.

Do you support dark mode?

Yes—cinematic dark styling is the default. The entire portal is designed with a premium dark aesthetic that's easy on the eyes during long gaming sessions and looks great on any screen.

Do I need an account?

This site is presented as a simple portal experience. No sign-ups, no passwords, no profiles. Just click and play. We believe gaming should be instant and hassle-free for students.

How fast do you respond?

Usually within 1–2 school days. We know students have limited free time, so we prioritize quick responses to questions, suggestions, and technical issues. Reach out anytime.
